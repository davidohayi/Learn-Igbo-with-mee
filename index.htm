Meet Your Instructor, Onye Nkuzi Igbo

Nnoo!(Welcome!)I’m Onye Nkuzi Igbo.I am a passionate linguist and a proud daughter of Igboland. My mission is to share the beauty of our language and culture with the world in a way that is modern, engaging, and most importantly fun. Whether you are a complete beginner looking to reconnect with your roots or someone wanting to sharpen your conversational skills, I am here to guide you every step of the way. Let's bring the soul of West Africa to your tongue.
